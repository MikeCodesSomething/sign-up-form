Demo form

This is a example form. It doesn't send the data anywhere, but still, don't put your actual details for anything in any of the boxes.. please!

This form does have some custom validation logic, so feel free to play with the fields with some dummy data.

    Your password must:
  • Have 8+ characters
  • Have 1+ lower case letter
  • Have 1+ upper case letter
  • Have 1+ number
  • Have 1+ special character eg. !$%&
  • Match the confirm password field
